500g sugar
250g 80% chocolate
100g poblano pepper
50g butter
200g heavy cream

Recipe: 
1- mince pepper
2- add pepper, cream and sugar to a heavy pot
3- cook until the fudge hits 236 deg f.
4- add butter and chocolate. 
5- mix lightly and pour into a parchement paper covered pan. 
6- cool off for 6 hours
