Ingredients:


1 pouch Betty Crocker* Sugar cookie mix

1/3 cup (75 mL) unsweetened baking cocoa

3 tbsp (45 mL) instant coffee granules or crystals

½ cup (125 mL) butter or margarine, softened

3 tbsp (45 mL) hazelnut-flavoured syrup for beverages

1 egg

1 ½ cups (375 mL) toasted hazelnuts, chopped

1 cup (250 mL) miniature semisweet chocolate chips

2/3 cup (150 mL) Betty Crocker* Creamy Deluxe* Frosting (any chocolate flavour)

4 tsp (20 mL) hazelnut-flavoured syrup for beverages


Method:

Heat oven to 350°F.
In large bowl, stir together cookie mix, cocoa and instant coffee.
Add butter, 3 tbsp (45 mL) syrup and the egg; stir until soft dough forms.
Stir in 1 cup (250 mL) of the nuts and the chocolate chips.

On ungreased baking sheets, drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches (5 cm) apart.
Press each mound to flatten slightly.

Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until set.
Cool 3 minutes; remove from baking sheets to cooling racks.
Cool completely, about 15 minutes.

In small bowl, stir frosting and 4 tsp (20 mL) syrup.
Spread about 1 tsp (5 mL) frosting on each cookie.
Sprinkle with remaining ½ cup (125 mL) nuts.
